🎮 Picto Play

Privacy Policy — Last updated: July 2026

Summary: Picto Play does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All content (photos, audio recordings) is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

1. Who We Are

Picto Play is a visual communication and learning aid developed by sidj-pd. It is designed to support children with ASD (Autism Spectrum Disorder) and ADHD through picture-based word association and audio playback.

Contact: sidnikj@gmail.com

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Picto Play does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any data about the child using the app

3. Microphone / Audio Recording

Picto Play requests the RECORD_AUDIO permission solely so caregivers can record spoken words or phrases to associate with picture flashcards.

  • Audio recordings are saved only on your device in the app's private storage.
  • Recordings are never uploaded, streamed, or shared with any server or third party.
  • You can delete recordings at any time by editing or removing a card within the app.

4. Photos / Images

Picto Play accesses photos from your device gallery when you choose to add a picture to a flashcard. This access is read-only and only triggered by an explicit action from the caregiver.

  • Photos are referenced locally on your device and are never transmitted externally.
  • No copies of your photos are made outside the app's storage.

5. In-App Purchases (Donations)

Picto Play offers optional tip/donation purchases via Google Play Billing. These transactions are processed entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • We do not store or access any payment card or billing information.
  • Donation purchases are protected by your device's screen lock (PIN/password/pattern).

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Picto Play is designed for use by and with children, operated by their caregivers. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, or from anyone at all. The app functions entirely offline and does not communicate with any external server.

7. Internet Access

Picto Play does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests except those initiated by the Google Play Billing library when processing a voluntary donation.

8. Third-Party SDKs

The only third-party library used is:

  • Google Play Billing (v7) — used only when a caregiver initiates a donation. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

There are no advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking libraries of any kind.

9. Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there is no data in transit to protect. Audio files and image references are stored in the app's private internal storage, which is only accessible to the app itself on non-rooted devices.

10. Your Rights

Because we collect no data, there is nothing to access, rectify, or delete on our end. All app content (recordings, panels, cards) can be deleted directly within the app or by uninstalling it.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "last updated" date. We encourage caregivers to review it periodically.
